Stranger Documentation

Welcome to the documentation for Stranger, a regular character living in the world of your comic alongside Stickman, Jib, and others.

Overview

Stranger is an ordinary figure in the Pencil Earth Dimension, a world of grayscale sketches born from the gamma ray burst. He’s just a guy you’d pass by in the comic’s bustling panels, blending into the everyday life of this unique universe.

Stranger’s Story

Stranger is the kind of character who feels like he’s always been around in your comic. Back on the original Earth, he was an average person—maybe a shop clerk, a neighbor, or someone waiting at a bus stop. When the gamma ray burst transformed the world into the Pencil Earth Dimension, Stranger adapted without much fuss. Now, he’s a familiar face in the comic’s towns and paths, always ready with a nod or a quick chat.

In the comic, Stranger pops up in the background or in small scenes, adding life to the world. He might be seen trading goods at a market, sketching a map for a traveler, or just leaning against a wall, watching Stickman or Jib pass by. His story isn’t grand or dramatic—he’s content being part of the everyday rhythm of the Pencil Earth, making the comic feel lived-in and real.

Appearance

Stranger’s design is simple and unremarkable, fitting his role:

  • Body: A basic humanoid shape with soft, rounded edges, drawn in clean grayscale lines.
  • Head: A plain oval with minimal features—just enough to show a friendly expression.
  • Clothing: Wears simple, nondescript clothes, like a loose tunic or jacket, typical of the Pencil Earth’s sketch-like aesthetic.

His look is deliberately generic, making him a versatile background character in the comic.

Evolution
